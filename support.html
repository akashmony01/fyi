Refund Policy

If you have made an error in making your donation or change your mind about contributing to The FYI, we will honor your request for a refund made within 15 business days of your donation. To request a refund, email us at info (at) thefyi.org. Refunds are returned using the original method of payment, except cash donations will be returned via check. If you made your donation by credit card, your refund will be credited to that same credit card.

Privacy Policy

The FYI respects and protects your privacy. The FYI will never sell or rent your personal information to other individuals or companies. When you make a donation, your email address will be included in The FYI monthly newsletter announcement list. If you do not wish to hear from us, simply unsubscribe from the list. The link to unsubscribe is at the bottom of each of the emails you receive from us.

When you make a donation with The FYI you will receive an acknowledgment of your donation for your records, and occasional email and postal delivery updates. If you do not wish to hear from us, simply unsubscribe from any mailing (as described above) or contact us directly.

Tax receipts are usually sent out at the beginning of each calendar year for donations made in the previous calendar year. If you have questions about your donations or the options available please contact us.
